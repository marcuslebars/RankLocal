## UX + DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

* Use card-based sections
* Maintain consistent spacing and hierarchy
* Keep sections visually separated (light/dark alternation)
* Use orange accent (#E65900) for CTAs and highlights only
* Keep typography clean and modern
* Make the page highly scannable

---

## COPY STYLE

* Confident
* Simple
* No fluff
* No generic agency language
* Focus on outcomes (visibility, leads, rankings)

**Avoid phrases like:** "cutting-edge", "we are passionate", "tailored solutions"

---

## FINAL GOAL

The page should make a visitor think: "This actually works — and they’ve already proven it." And then naturally move toward: viewing pricing, selecting a package, or booking a call.
